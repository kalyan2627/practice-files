Celebrating Customer Service Week

Customer Centricity has always been HDB's top priority to bolster this strong facet, Finincial Bank has launched an innovative initiative called "Customer Service Week". This initiative follows a pattern of CSW celebration - 4 times a year in each branch. To add further, the initiative aims to create awareness amongst walk in customers on Finincial bank's various Self -Service tools that customers can use to manage their loan account or apply for a new loan, digital payments options available, grievance mechanism and RBI's Internal Ombudsman Scheme A customer feedback is also be taken to gauge the quality of service provided and expected, bringing in continual improvement.

Our team of experienced professionals works with dedication to understand customer needs and provide tailored financial solutions. We believe in long-term relationships, ethical practices, and responsible banking that supports community growth and economic development. Driven by innovation and guided by values, we strive to be a dependable financial partner for every stage of our customers’ journey.
