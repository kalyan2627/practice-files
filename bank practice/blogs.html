When you need a significant amount of money for personal or business needs, finding the right financing option can make all the difference. A **Loan Against Property (LAP)** allows you to leverage the value of real estate you already own to secure funds at relatively lower interest rates compared to unsecured loans. LAP can provide both the scale and flexibility you need to either expand your business, fund higher education, consolidate (restructure) your debts, or cover any medical expenses. How Does An LAP Loan Work? A **loan against property** is a secured loan where you pledge residential, commercial, or industrial property as collateral. The lender evaluates the property’s market value and offers a percentage of that value as the final loan amount. Since the property acts as security, lenders are more willing to offer larger amounts with longer repayment tenures and competitive **interest rates**.
